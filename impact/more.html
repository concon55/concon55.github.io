Inspiration

Around the world, people need help. People have needs. How can we reach these people? What can we do to help?

I asked myself these questions often because new things happen constantly- locally, nationally, internationally. In times of crisis, I want to know how I can contribute quickly.

What organizations are trustworthy? Which organizations will directly help the cause that I support?

I needed answers. So I created Impact.
